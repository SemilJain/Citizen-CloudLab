Reducing Flight Cancellations

Project Title


Project Description

Have you ever experienced a flight cancellation or delay due to unforeseen weather conditions? Now you can contribute to reducing flight disruptions by providing valuable data through our questionnaire form. Share details about your flight, including dates, source, destination, and any weather-related issues encountered. By analyzing this information alongside weather forecasts, we aim to determine if weather disruptions could have been predicted earlier, potentially avoiding cancellations and delays in the future.

Spot the Tornado

Project Title


Project Description

Join us in our mission to enhance tornado detection capabilities! Our tool provides an interactive learning experience where you can train to spot tornados from images generated by weather models. Gain insights into tornado formation, characteristics, and identification techniques while contributing to tornado detection research. Together, we can improve tornado awareness and preparedness in vulnerable communities.

About Citizen-CloudLab

Meet the Minds Behind Citizen-CloudLab.

Citizen-CloudLab pioneers climate citizen science by federating with the National Discovery Cloud for Climate (NDC-C). Our platform provides accessible environments for individuals and groups to engage with climate models, empowering them to contribute to scientific discoveries.

Led by CloudLab PI Robert Ricci and a dedicated team at the University of Utah, Citizen-CloudLab aims to democratize access to climate science resources and inspire future generations of scientists.

  • Pioneering Climate Citizen Science: Accessible science for everyone.
  • Collaboration with NDC-C: Uniting for climate research advancement.
  • Commitment to Openness: Transparent progress for all.
